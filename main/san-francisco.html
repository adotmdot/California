San Francisco's famous attractions

Region of the State Where San Francisco is Located

San Francisco, located in Northern California, is a major cultural, financial, and technological hub on the West Coast of the United States. Situated on a hilly peninsula between the Pacific Ocean and San Francisco Bay, it is known for its iconic landmarks, including the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz Island, and cable cars. With a mild, coastal climate, the city experiences cool summers, frequent fog, and moderate winters. As a global financial center, it hosts the headquarters of major corporations and startups, particularly in technology, finance, and tourism. Home to diverse communities, world-class universities, and a rich history, San Francisco is a dynamic metropolis blending innovation, culture, and natural beauty.
